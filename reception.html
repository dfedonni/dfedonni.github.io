The Reception 5pm-10pm(after party 10pm-1am)

The reception is located about a mile west of the ceremony at the Racquet Club of Philadelphia, 215 S 16th St, Philadelphia, PA 19102.

Cocktail hour at the reception will start 30 minutes after the ceremony around 5pm. Hors d'oeuvres and drinks will be served while the wedding party finishes up with photos and makes thier way to the reception. Immediately following the reception we will host an after party in the basement-level lounge for anyone who would like to continue the celebration!

Dress Code While the racquet club requires all guest to adhere to "smart casual" attire, for the reception we encourage a more formal attire.

COVID Policy Please bring proof of vaccination to the reption. According to city regulations, only guest who provide proof of vaccination are allowed to attend.
