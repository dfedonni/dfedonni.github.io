Ceremony

The wedding ceremony will begin at 3:30pm at Old St. Joesph's Church. Old St. Joesph's is located at 321 Willings Alley, Philadelphia, PA 19106.

From the recommended hotels the ceremony is roughly 10 blocks away depending on the weather it could be a lovely walk.
